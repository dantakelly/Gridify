G R I D S

Grids Of Four

The 'Grids of 4' constructer comprises a structural arrangement characterized by the division of a
container into discrete sections, each housing a quartet of content elements. Employing
cascading style sheets (CSS), these grids are endowed with stylistic attributes to enhance visual
coherence and user experience.

Grids Of Eight

The 'Grids of 8' architecture embodies a structural design characterized by dividing a container
into distinct segments, each housing an octet of content elements.
